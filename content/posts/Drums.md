This is a drumkit which is **very cool**.

> It has 7 different *hitty things*.

- Hi-Hat
- Snare
- Ride
- Kick
- High-Tom
- Mid-Tom
- Floor-Tom

It is missing a crash symbol and therefore is only a 7/10.
